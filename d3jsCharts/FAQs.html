Frequently Asked Questions

Is cyber insurance expensive?

Not always. Premiums vary based on business size, industry, and your cyber posture.

Do I need it if I already have IT security tools?

Yes—tools help prevent incidents, but insurance helps when prevention fails.

What if I don’t understand the policy wording?

Ask your broker. You can also use our glossary to decode terms.

Can I be denied coverage?

Yes, if your cyber security practices are too weak or you misrepresent information.
