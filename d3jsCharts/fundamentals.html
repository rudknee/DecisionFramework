Cyber Insurance 101

Cyber insurance protects your business from the financial impact of cyber incidents like:

  • Ransomware attacks
  • Data breaches
  • Business interruption
  • Legal liabilities from leaked data

Common Types of Cover

  • First-party: Costs related to your own losses (e.g. recovery costs, lost revenue).
  • Third-party: Costs from claims against you (e.g. legal fees, customer notifications).

Why Cyber Insurance Matters for SMEs

SMEs are not too small to be targeted. In fact, attackers often prefer them.

  • Over 60% of SMEs experience a cyber incident each year.
  • The average recovery cost can exceed $100,000.
  • Many SMEs close down within six months after a major breach.

Cyber insurance can mean the difference between survival and shutdown. It helps you respond faster, limit damage, and avoid legal or reputational disasters.
