Cyber Security Guide

The guide below is a question-based flowchart to aid decision-makers in thinking through their organization's cyber security posture and potential need for cyber insurance.

The guide aims not to be overly prescriptive and does not claim to cover all use-cases. Rather, it seeks to support decision-making by helping the user explore which actions could be beneficial to their use case. Whether a particular solution is suitable will depend on the specific context, and results from the Guide should be read as suggestions to explore rather than definitive solutions.

It is also important to understand that using an individual tool or control does not in itself guarantee security unless accompanied by a good overall security design and appropriate governance. You can explore good practices here.

Using the guide

  • The guide is primarily aimed at business owners, IT managers, and decision-makers.
  • Answer the questions that appear in the decision tree by clicking on the box containing the appropriate answer. Do this until you reach an end node which will suggest a course of action.
  • Each end node can be clicked to lead to a page that describes the suggested action in more detail.
  • You can explore the whole decision tree structure by clicking to expand the various different nodes in the tree.
  • Click and drag within the black box to pan through the tree diagram, and use your mouse wheel or trackpad to zoom in and out.
